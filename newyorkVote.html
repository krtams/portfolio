New York
In New York a person must be registered to vote at least 25 days before the actual election date. A person can either register to vote online at the New York DMV website, in person at your New York County Board of Elections, or through sending a completed New York Registration Form to your New York County Board of Elections. To vote in the primary election which determines the candidate that will represent each party, a person must be a registered member of that party. The next general election is November 6, 2018; it will fill the Senate seat currently held by Kirsten Gillibrand. Go to this page to register to vote online. A person only needs to register once unless they change their name or change their address.
